Privacy Policy — Nextup

Last updated: July 2026

Nextup ("the App") is a TV show tracking application. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, used to create and secure your account.
  • Watch history: the shows and episodes you mark as watched, planned, or dropped, and any ratings you provide.
  • Imported data (optional): if you choose to import your TV Time export files, we process the show titles, episode counts, and ratings contained in those files to populate your watchlist. These files are processed to build your library and are not retained beyond what's needed to complete the import.

How we use this information

Your data is used solely to provide the App's core functionality: tracking what you watch, showing upcoming episodes, and syncing your library across devices. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — hosts our database and handles authentication. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — we use TMDB's API to retrieve show information, images, and cast details. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. See TMDB's privacy policy.

Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
